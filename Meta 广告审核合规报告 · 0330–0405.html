三、审核灰区（Tier 2）
437 条灰区违规（5.3%）— 存在风险但暂未触红线，建议优化调整
GRAY-A
轻量健康/功效暗示
~186
功效表述使用模糊语言，暗示健康效果但未直接声称，介于合规与违规边界
"Supports your skin's natural renewal process"（暗示性功效）
"Many customers noticed improvements in..." （未明确声称但有功效倾向）
GRAY-B
诱导性互动
~108
文案中引导点赞、评论、分享，以互动行为换取抽奖或优惠资格
"Tag a friend who needs this gift!"
"Comment 'YES' below and we'll send you the link!"
GRAY-C
资质/认证声明待核实
~87
声称具备医学认证、医生推荐等权威背书，但未提交对应资质文件
"Dermatologist-tested formula"（测试报告未上传）
"As seen on [media outlet]"（未提供报道链接）
GRAY-D
落地页信息不一致
~56

广告承诺"30-day return"，落地页只有 14 天退换政策
本期灰区占比从上期 7.2% 降至 5.3%，注意：这不一定意味着合规改善——部分灰区内容可能因 Meta 政策收紧而直接升级为 Tier 3 红线。建议重点排查 GRAY-A（轻量功效暗示），是最容易升级为 Prohibited Health Claims 的类型。

四、红线 vs 灰区对照表
判定边界说明 — 帮助 AM 区分哪些表述触红线，哪些属于灰区可优化
违规类型 🚫 Tier 3 — 必须整改（典型表述） ⚡ Tier 2 — 灰区（边界表述） ✅ 合规表述
健康功效 "Cures acne in 3 days" / "Treats eczema" "Helps with skin concern..." / "Supports healing" "May help reduce the appearance of blemishes"
医美宣称 "No need for Botox! This works better!" / "Alternative to fillers" "Like a facelift at home" (暗示) "A relaxing skincare routine for fine lines"
身体形象 "Fix your embarrassing [body part]" / "Don't be ashamed anymore" "Feel more confident about your [area]" "Designed to help you feel comfortable and at ease"
疾病治疗 "Prevents diabetes" / "Eliminates cancer cells" "Supports blood sugar balance" (暗示功能) "As part of a healthy lifestyle, may support overall wellness"
虚假紧迫 "Only 3 left!" (实际充足) / "Sale ends in 10min!" (循环) "Limited-time offer" (无具体截止时间) "Special offer available while supplies last"
成分夸大 "100% natural — zero side effects" / "Proven by science" "With powerful natural ingredients" (模糊) "Contains Vitamin C, known to support skin brightness"
体重/减脂 "Lose 10kg in 7 days guaranteed!" / "Melts fat overnight" "Supports weight management journey" (暗示) "May help support a healthy metabolism with diet & exercise"
商业欺骗 虚高原价打折 / "FREE" 实为订阅 优惠描述不够清晰、条件隐藏 清晰标注折扣条件、原价来源
权威背书 "FDA approved" (未获批) / "Doctor recommended" (无来源) "Dermatologist-tested" (未上传证明) "Formulated with dermatologist input" (需提供资质)

八、AM 合规操作手册
基于本期数据更新的改写原则、高危词黑名单与上线前自检清单
改写原则（本期重点：Health / Beauty 品类）
✅ 允许的表达方式
"May help support skin hydration" — 弱化功效，使用 may/might/can help

"Contains Vitamin C, which is known to..." — 成分逻辑推导

"Many customers reported noticing improvements in..." — 个体化表述

"Designed as part of a wellness routine" — 场景化描述而非功效声明

"Results may vary — see individual testimonials*" — 效果图附带免责声明
🚫 禁止的表达方式（本期高频违规）
"No need for Botox / fillers" — 医美替代宣称（H4a，1,375 条）

"Cures / treats / reverses [condition]" — 疾病治疗声明（H2a，824 条）

"Guaranteed to [body transformation]" — 身体变化承诺（H1d，2,001 条）

"Only X left — final sale!" — 虚假紧迫感（46 条）

"Doctor recommended" + 未提交资质 — 未证实权威背书
